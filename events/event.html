Sat, Feb 14 • 8:00 PM

Jazz Under the Stars

Skyline Rooftop Garden, New York

About the Event

Join us for an unforgettable evening of smooth jazz and breathtaking city views. "Jazz Under the Stars" features the renowned Quartet Noir performing a special set of classic standards and original compositions.

Located at the exclusive Skyline Rooftop Garden, you'll enjoy a curated selection of cocktails and hors d'oeuvres while soaking in the ambiance of the city lights. Perfect for a romantic Valentine's date or a sophisticated night out with friends.

Doors open at 7:00 PM. Performance begins at 8:00 PM. Semi-formal attire tailored to the weather is recommended.

Lineup

  • Quartet Noir (Headliner)
  • Sarah Vaughn Tribute Trio (Opener)

Venue Details

The Skyline Rooftop Garden offers panoramic views of the entire city. It is fully accessible via elevator. In case of rain, the event will be moved to the glass-enclosed atrium.
